What We Do:

At Tealaa Innovations, we are revolutionizing the aquaculture industry by developing cutting-edge technologies that enhance productivity and sustainability in traditional earthen ponds. Our focus is on creating autonomous robotic solutions for efficient sludge removal, enabling farmers to maximize their yields and maintain healthier aquatic environments.

The Problem:

Traditional earthen aquaculture ponds suffer from high organic load and inefficient sludge management, leading to disease outbreaks, reduced stocking density, and compromised water quality. These issues contribute to significant losses in productivity and profitability for farmers, particularly in regions relying on outdated aquaculture practices.

Our Product:

We have developed an autonomous sludge removal robot designed specifically for aquaculture ponds. This innovative system selectively removes sludge without disturbing the pond bottom, drastically reducing the organic load and improving overall pond health. By addressing the root causes of disease outbreaks, our product enables farmers to increase their stocking density and achieve higher productivity.
